Who We Are

Introduction to InAmigos

Founded on September 23, 2020, by Govind Shukla, the InAmigos Foundation is a registered Section 8 non-profit organization based in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. We believe in the power of collective action to bring about meaningful change. Through our various initiatives, we strive to build a more equitable, educated, and sustainable society for all. We hold 80G and 12A certifications, CSR-1 registration, and are ISO 9001:2015 certified.

What We Do

Our Ongoing Projects

Humanitarian

Project SEVA

Focuses on food and clothing distribution for the underprivileged, ensuring basic necessities reach those who need them most.

Education

Project BACHPANSHALA

Bridging educational gaps for underprivileged children by providing support in school education, digital literacy, and essential life skills.

Environment

Project PRAKRITI

An environmental sustainability initiative focused on massive tree planting campaigns and supporting eco-friendly agriculture practices.

Empowerment

Project UDAAN

Empowering women through collaboration with rural self-help groups, promoting financial independence and menstrual hygiene awareness.

Animal Welfare

Project JEEV

Dedicated to animal welfare across the country, including active rescue, protection, and feeding efforts for stray and abandoned animals.

Skill Development

Project VIKAS

A youth skill development initiative offering valuable internships in fields like data operations, finance, digital marketing, and social work.

Our Purpose

Driving Real Social Impact

At InAmigos Foundation, our purpose goes beyond temporary relief. We aim for sustainable development and long-term empowerment. Our holistic approach tackles interconnected issues—from lack of education and poverty to environmental degradation.

  • Empowering the Next Generation: Through #Bachpanshala and #Vikas, we are preparing youth for a brighter future.
  • Protecting Our Planet: #Prakriti drives environmental consciousness and active greening.
  • Compassion for All: #Jeev ensures that voiceless animals are cared for and protected.
  • Uplifting Women: #Udaan creates financial independence and breaks social stigmas.
